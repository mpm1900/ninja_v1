# Main Cast: Playstyles, Roles, and Signature Moves

## Naruto Uzumaki
- **Role:** All-rounder, burst attacker, field control
- **Playstyle:** Versatile; can deal heavy damage, buff self, summon, and use clones for tactical advantage.
- **Signature Moves:** Rasengan, Rasenshuriken, Shadow Clone, Sage Mode, Summon Gamabunta

## Sasuke Uchiha
- **Role:** Fast special attacker, finisher, disruption
- **Playstyle:** High speed and chakra attack; excels at single-target burst, status, and field control.
- **Signature Moves:** Chidori, Kirin, Amaterasu, Great Fireball, Chidori Stream

## Sakura Haruno
- **Role:** Tank, healer, support
- **Playstyle:** High attack/defense, healing, and team buffs; can disrupt and control the field.
- **Signature Moves:** Cherry Blossom Impact, Team Heal, Mud Wall, Sage Mode

## Kakashi Hatake
- **Role:** Versatile technician, copycat, counterplay
- **Playstyle:** Wide movepool, can copy enemy jutsu, strong lightning/earth attacks, counters and disables.
- **Signature Moves:** Raikiri, Kamui, Copy Jutsu, Lightning Hound, Mud Wall

## Itachi Uchiha
- **Role:** Debuffer, controller, special attacker
- **Playstyle:** Status infliction, disables, high chakra damage, battlefield manipulation.
- **Signature Moves:** Amaterasu, Tsukuyomi (suggested), Phoenix Flower, Pattern Break, Mirage Crow

## Jiraiya
- **Role:** Bulky attacker, summoner, field control
- **Playstyle:** Strong AoE, summons, status, and Sage Mode for power spikes.
- **Signature Moves:** Rasengan, Giant Rasengan, Sage Mode, Summon Gamabunta, Flame Bullet

## Orochimaru
- **Role:** Disruptor, self-healer, status inflicter
- **Playstyle:** Versatile, can heal, inflict status, and use unique snake-based attacks.
- **Signature Moves:** Snake Strike, Body Replacement, Pattern Break, Mind Transfer, Recover
